About Me

“How many programmers does it take to change a light bulb? None, that’s a hardware problem.”

Jokes aside, I'm a budding software developer with a background in Biotechnology. With the combination of these two disciplines, you can definitely tell that I have a thirst for knowledge!

I started out in as a chemistry analyst responsible for QA testing. Feeling frustrated with laboratory software, I started to dabble in coding and program development to see if I could make a difference. The vast amount of new technology and the endless possibilities of programming led me to pursue the computer programming course at Conestoga College while still working as a full time analyst. Two years later, as a graduate, I am overwhelmed with the possibilities!

  • Bachelor of Science, majoring in Biotechnology
  • Diploma in Computer Programming
  • A life long learner, excited about using new technologies to make a difference in the world!
